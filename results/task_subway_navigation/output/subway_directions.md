# Subway Directions: Bedford Ave (L) → Yankee Stadium (161 St, 4)

**Date:** July 16, 2026
**From:** Bedford Ave (L train, Williamsburg, Brooklyn)
**To:** 161 St-Yankee Stadium (4 train, Bronx)

---

## Recommended Route: L → 4 (via 14 St-Union Sq)

This is the fastest and most direct option — one transfer at a major Manhattan hub.

### Step-by-Step Directions

1. **Board the L train** at Bedford Ave station in Williamsburg. Take the **Manhattan-bound (8 Ave)** train (heading west toward 8 Ave-14 St).
2. **Ride the L for 3 stops:**
   - Bedford Ave → 1 Ave → 3 Ave → **14 St-Union Sq**
3. **Transfer at 14 St-Union Sq** to the **uptown 4 train** (heading north toward Woodlawn in the Bronx). Follow signs for the "Uptown 4/5/6" platform.
4. **Ride the uptown 4 for 6 stops:**
   - 14 St-Union Sq → Grand Central-42 St → 59 St → 86 St → 125 St → 149 St-Grand Concourse → **161 St-Yankee Stadium**
5. **Exit** at 161 St-Yankee Stadium. The stadium is directly adjacent to the station; follow signs for Yankee Stadium exits (usually the River Ave / 161 St exits).

### Stops per Leg

| Leg | Line | Stops |
|---|---|---|
| Bedford Ave → 14 St-Union Sq | L (Manhattan-bound) | 3 stops |
| 14 St-Union Sq → 161 St-Yankee Stadium | 4 (Uptown/Express) | 6 stops |
| **Total** | | **9 stops** |

### Estimated Travel Time

- **Stops:** 9 stops × ~2.5 minutes (mix of local on L and express on 4) ≈ **20–25 minutes**
- **Transfer at Union Sq:** ~5–8 minutes (relatively straightforward platform-to-platform transfer, but can be crowded)
- **Walking + waiting/padding:** ~5 minutes
- **Total estimated travel time: ~30–40 minutes**

*Plan on closer to 40–45 minutes during rush hour (7–10 AM / 5–7 PM weekdays) due to crowded trains and slower transfers.*

## Alternative Route: L → 2 → 4 (via 14th St complex + 149 St-Grand Concourse)

Use this as a backup if there are 4 train delays or service changes on the Lexington Ave line. It adds one more transfer but uses the 7th Ave express instead.

### Step-by-Step Directions

1. **Board the Manhattan-bound L** at Bedford Ave.
2. **Stay on the L past Union Sq** — ride it all the way to its Manhattan terminus at **8 Ave-14 St** (5 stops from Bedford Ave: 1 Ave → 3 Ave → Union Sq → 6 Ave → 8 Ave-14 St).
3. **Walk through the connecting passageway** from 14 St-8th Ave (L/A/C/E) to **14 St-7th Ave** (1/2/3). Signs will point toward "1/2/3 — 7th Ave." This is an underground in-system walk of ~3–5 minutes.
4. **Board the uptown 2 train** (express, heading toward Wakefield-241 St).
5. **Ride the 2 for 9 stops** north into the Bronx:
   - 14 St-7th Ave → 34 St-Penn Station → Times Sq-42 St → 72 St → 96 St → Central Park North-110 St → 116 St → 125 St → 135 St → **149 St-Grand Concourse**
6. **Transfer at 149 St-Grand Concourse** to the **uptown 4 train** (one stop north).
7. **Ride the 4 for 1 stop** to **161 St-Yankee Stadium**.

### Stops per Leg (Alternative)

| Leg | Line | Stops |
|---|---|---|
| Bedford Ave → 8 Ave-14 St | L | 5 stops |
| 14 St-7th Ave → 149 St-Grand Concourse | 2 (Uptown/Express) | 9 stops |
| 149 St-Grand Concourse → 161 St-Yankee Stadium | 4 | 1 stop |
| **Total** | | **15 stops** |

### Estimated Travel Time (Alternative)

- Stops: 15 × ~2.5 min ≈ **35–40 minutes**
- Two transfers: 8–10 min (14th St walkway) + 5 min (149 St) ≈ **13–18 minutes**
- **Total: ~50–60 minutes** (not faster, but useful as a backup)

## Tips

### Best Car Position for Easier Transfers

- **At Bedford Ave (boarding the L):** For the transfer at Union Sq to the 4 train, ride in the **front of the L train** (toward the 8 Ave end of the platform). The exit stairs at Union Sq's L platform closest to the Lexington Ave (4/5/6) mezzanine are toward the front of the Manhattan-bound train, shortening your walk to the 4.
- **If taking the alternative route (L → 2 at 14th St):** Ride in the **rear of the L train** (back of the Manhattan-bound train) to be closest to the passageway leading from 8 Ave-14 St toward 14 St-7th Ave.
- **On the uptown 4 (from Union Sq):** For Yankee Stadium, ride in the **front half of the train** — the exit closest to Yankee Stadium (River Ave / Stadium side) is generally near the front of the northbound platform at 161 St.

### Rush Hour Considerations

- **Morning rush (7–10 AM, weekdays):** You're traveling opposite the main Brooklyn → Manhattan crush on the L, but the Union Sq transfer and the uptown Lexington 4 will be **very crowded** (Manhattan residents heading north to jobs in East Harlem / Bronx). Expect packed trains and add 5–10 minutes.
- **Evening rush (5–7 PM, weekdays):** The L into Manhattan will be moderately crowded; the uptown 4 is generally less crowded in the evening than morning, but still busy.
- **Yankees game days:** If traveling to an evening game (typical first pitch 7:05 PM), trains heading to 161 St fill up significantly starting around 5:30 PM, especially the 4 train from Grand Central north. Leave 15–20 minutes extra.
- **Weekend service:** Check MTA service advisories before you go — the 4 often runs local or skips stops on weekends due to maintenance, and the L has signal modernization work most weekends that can add wait time.

### General Tips

- **MetroCard / OMNY:** Use OMNY (tap-to-pay with phone or contactless card) at turnstiles — faster than buying a MetroCard.
- **Direction check:** Always confirm "Manhattan-bound" on the L platform in Williamsburg (signs point toward 8 Ave, not Canarsie-Rockaway Pkwy).
- **Signage:** At Union Sq, follow the green-circle "4" signs pointing uptown (toward Woodlawn / The Bronx) — **do not** get on the downtown 4 (toward Crown Hts-Utica Ave / Brooklyn), which goes the wrong way.
- **Stadium exit:** At 161 St-Yankee Stadium, exit toward **River Avenue and 161st Street** to surface right next to the stadium.
