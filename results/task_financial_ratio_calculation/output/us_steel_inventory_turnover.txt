U.S. Steel (United States Steel Corp., NYSE: X) — Inventory Turnover Ratio, Fiscal Year 2024
============================================================================================

Formula
-------
Inventory Turnover Ratio = Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) / Average Inventory

  where: Average Inventory = (Beginning Inventory + Ending Inventory) / 2

For a fiscal-year calculation, "beginning inventory" = inventory balance at the
end of the prior fiscal year (Dec 31, 2023), and "ending inventory" = inventory
balance at the end of the fiscal year in question (Dec 31, 2024).

Source: U.S. Steel Form 10-K for fiscal year ended December 31, 2024, filed
with the U.S. SEC on January 31, 2025; and comparative balance-sheet data from
the Form 10-K for fiscal year ended December 31, 2023 (filed January 31, 2024).
All figures in USD millions.

Values used
-----------
Cost of sales (the income-statement line item representing COGS; on U.S. Steel's
Consolidated Statements of Operations it is reported as "Cost of sales (excludes
items shown below)" because SG&A, DD&A and several other operating items are
broken out separately):

   Cost of sales, FY2024 ............ $14,060 million

Inventories, from the Consolidated Balance Sheets (also reconciled in Note 10,
"Inventories"):

   Inventories, Dec 31, 2023 ........ $ 2,128 million
   Inventories, Dec 31, 2024 ........ $ 2,168 million
   Average Inventory, FY2024 ........ ($2,128 + $2,168) / 2 = $2,148 million

Note on inventory composition (Note 10, Dec 31, 2024): raw materials $959M,
semi-finished products $828M, finished products $331M, supplies & sundry $50M
= $2,168M total.

Calculation
-----------
Inventory Turnover Ratio (FY2024) = $14,060M / $2,148M
                                  ≈ 6.55 times per year

Implied Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO) = 365 / 6.55 ≈ 55.8 days

For reference, using ending inventory instead of average inventory:
   Turnover = $14,060M / $2,168M ≈ 6.49×  (~56.3 days)

Result
------
U.S. Steel inventory turnover ratio, fiscal year 2024 ≈ 6.55× (≈ 6.5 times)
