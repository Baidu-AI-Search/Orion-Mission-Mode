GitLab CFO as of April 7, 2026
==============================

Chief Financial Officer (CFO): Jessica Ross

Source note / currency reference (as of April 7, 2026):
- Brian Robins, GitLab's long-serving CFO, announced his departure alongside the
  company's FY2026 Q2 (calendar Q3 2025) earnings release on September 4, 2025,
  with an effective departure date of September 19, 2025.
- Jessica Ross was introduced as GitLab's new Chief Financial Officer on
  GitLab's FY2026 Q4 (fiscal year ending January 31, 2026) earnings call,
  held on March 3, 2026 and publicly summarized on March 5, 2026. She was
  explicitly identified as CFO and "首次作为CFO出席" (attending for the first
  time as CFO), presenting FY2026 Q4 / full-year results (full-year revenue
  $955M, +26% YoY; free cash flow $220M, +83% YoY) and FY2027 guidance
  alongside CEO Bill Staples.
- She continued to serve as CFO in subsequent public appearances, including
  the May 26, 2026 investor Q&A session where she again spoke on record as
  GitLab's Chief Financial Officer.
- No subsequent CFO transition announcement was found between March 3, 2026
  and April 7, 2026, confirming Jessica Ross as the sitting CFO of GitLab
  as of April 7, 2026.
