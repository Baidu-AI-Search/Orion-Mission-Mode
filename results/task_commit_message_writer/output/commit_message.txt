feat(auth): add remember-me sessions and session refresh

Users were being logged out after one hour of inactivity, causing
friction on repeat visits and forcing them to re-authenticate
unnecessarily. Trusted devices need a way to stay signed in across
extended periods, and active sessions should be able to extend their
lifetime without requiring a fresh login.

Add an optional rememberMe flag to createSession that stores sessions
with a 30-day TTL, while preserving the existing 1-hour default for
standard logins. Introduce refreshSession to reset a session's TTL
back to its original lifetime, so callers can slide the expiration on
activity. Cover both behaviors with unit tests, including the
nonexistent-token edge case.
