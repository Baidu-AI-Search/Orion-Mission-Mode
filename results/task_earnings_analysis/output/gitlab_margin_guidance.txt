Quarter identification
----------------------

corresponds to GitLab's fiscal Q3 FY2026 (three months ended October 31,
2025), reported on December 2, 2025. The guidance for this quarter was
issued alongside Q2 FY2026 results on September 3, 2025.

Guided margin (Q3 FY2026, issued 9/3/2025)
------------------------------------------
GitLab guides to non-GAAP operating income in dollars (not to an explicit
margin percentage), alongside a revenue range:
  - Revenue guidance:          $238.0M – $239.0M  (midpoint $238.5M)
  - Non-GAAP operating income:  $31.0M –  $32.0M  (midpoint  $31.5M)
  - Implied non-GAAP operating margin (midpoint): $31.5M / $238.5M ≈ 13.2%
  - High end of implied guidance: $32.0M / $238.0M ≈ 13.4%
  - Rounded (consistent with GitLab's whole-percent presentation): ~13%

Actual margin (Q3 FY2026, reported 12/2/2025)
---------------------------------------------
  - Revenue:                   $244.4M
  - Non-GAAP operating income:  $43.7M
  - Non-GAAP operating margin as reported by GitLab in the Q3 highlights
    table: 18%
  - Calculated: $43.7M / $244.4M ≈ 17.9% (rounds to 18% using GitLab's
    rounding convention)

Beat / (miss) versus guidance
-----------------------------
  - Actual non-GAAP operating margin: 18%
  - Guided non-GAAP operating margin (implied): ~13%
  - Beat: approximately 5 percentage points, i.e., ~500 basis points
    (~467 bps versus the unrounded midpoint; ~444 bps versus the high end
    of the implied dollar range; ~500 bps on the rounded basis that GitLab
    uses in its reported tables and that analyst write-ups cite).
